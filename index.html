The Orca (orcinus orca, killer whale, blackfish)

Orcas are apex predators, meaning they themselves have no natural predators.

They are social animals and hunt in groups, like wolves of the sea. These groups are called pods, hunting small and large prey, from fish, seals, dolphins, whales and in one case, a great white shark in Hawaii.

The scientific name Orcinus means "of the kingdom of the dead."
